# Casino Scalper Pro

## Philosophy

Casino Scalper Pro is an institutional trading assistant designed for Gold scalpers.

It does not predict the market.

It waits for confirmation.

The indicator follows this sequence:

HTF Bias
↓
Liquidity
↓
Sweep
↓
Displacement
↓
Confirmation
↓
Retest
↓
Entry
↓
Risk Management

---

## Design Principles

- Non-repainting
- Modular architecture
- One responsibility per module
- Gold-first optimisation
- Session-aware
- High quality setups only
